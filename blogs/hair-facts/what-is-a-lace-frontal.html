What is a Lace Frontal?

Why would I use a Lace Frontal?

One of the most popular ways to finish your protective style is a lace frontal. Perfect for style with no natural hair left out. With hairline coverage from ear to ear, you'll be able to part it anywhere and have complete versatility. Lace frontals are for finishing your weave hairstyle or when creating a wig.

What is a Lace Frontal?

It's a 13 x 4 inch lace hairpiece that covers the front hairline. This is perfect looking for protective styles with no hair left out. When installed correctly, it gives the illusion of hair growing from your scalp.

Is a lace frontal for me?

If you've never tried a lace frontal, imagine laying a thin piece of fabric along your hairline. It's comfortable and comes with baby hairs along the lace hairline. For less coverage, you could try a lace closure. For full hairline coverage, the 360 Lace Frontal is a great option. 

Feel free to reach out to us if this your first time using a silk closure. We're happy to answer any questions you have.  

How long will it last? 

Our hand tied lace frontal is a special piece for us. We take pride in giving you the most natural look possible. With proper care, you can expect 6 months to 1 year from one of our lace frontals with proper care.
